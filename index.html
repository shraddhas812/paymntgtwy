about us

Support the victims

A heart-wrenching train accident has taken place in Odisha, India, causing widespread depredation and despair. With over 290 lives lost and more than a thousand people injured, our hearts go out to the affected families and friends who are enduring unimaginable pain. Undoubtedly it is traumatic and devastating for both the survivors as well as those grieving the loss of their loved ones. Recovery and healing will require considerable amounts of time and collective efforts from everyone. During these difficult times, it is imperative that we unite together to lend our unwavering support to help rebiuld the lives of the victims of the deadly train accident.

Our Objectives

We aim to provide access to necessities like food, clean water, clothing, and temporary housing to the affected people.
We will also provide stability and hope for children who have lost their parents or guardians by offering support for their education.
Ensure that survivors receive continued medical attention, rehabilitation, and assistance as they pursue recovery and provide wheelchairs and artificial limbs as support aids for people with disabilities.
We will also support the families through the practical, emotional, and financial difficulties that they have encountered.

Our Camapign

We are a non-profit organisation and our primary goal is to engage actively and offer support during humanitarian crisis.
We have initiated a fundraising campaign to aid the victims and their families affected by this tragedy. The funds raised will be utilized to provide immediate assistance and essential support to those in need. We kindly ask for your support and generosity in joining us on this mission.
